Design Version 1

My MadLibs is a write-your-own midwest emo song! With this design, I wanted to evoke teenage angst and nostalgia for home, and decided to base my interface off of a retro desktop UI. The user will submit their answers through the 'terminal,' and the lyrics will populate in the music player. In addition to the form, I intend to add interactivity by making it possible for the user to bring each window to the front by clicking on it so they can explore the desktop of an angsty teenage boy!
